The Four Agreements

1. Be Impecable With Your Words.

- Speak with integrity.
- Don't use your words against yourself.
- Don't use your words against others.

2. Don't Take Anything Personally.

- When you are immune to the opinions and actions of others,
you won't be the victim of needless suffering.

3. Don't Make Assumptions.

- Find the courage to ask questions and to express what you really want.
- Communicate with others as clearly as you can to avoid
misunderstandings, sadness, and drama.

4. Always Do Your Best.

- Your best is going to change from moment to moment... from tired to rested.
- Under any circumstane, always do your best and you will avoid self-judgment, abuse, and regret.

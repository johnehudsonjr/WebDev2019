Sneak Peek

Synopsis

In the sequel to the dramatic comedy, Home Alone, Kevin McCallister, the kid who is accidentally left at home as his entire family traveled to Florida for vacation, and having to defend his home from burglars, finds himself left alone AGAIN, but this time in the big apple, New York City. Ironically, the same burglars are released from jail and are after him again, causing him to go back into defense mode to protect himself.

Distributed by 20th Century Fox
Release Date: November 20, 1992
Running Time: 120 minutes
